OCN: The Ontario Corporation Number (OCN) is the unique number assigned by the Ontario government to a corporation.

BIN: The Business Identification Number (BIN) is the unique number assigned by the Ontario government when you register a business name or partnership in Ontario.

NOTE: This should not be confused with the federal Business Number (BN) assigned by the Canada Revenue Agency for federal programs.
